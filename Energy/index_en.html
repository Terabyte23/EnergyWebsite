A new star on the Estonian market: energy drink "EnergyPulse" with guava extract and nanotechnology!

November 17, 2024

Recently, a presentation of a unique energy drink "EnergyPulse" took place in Tallinn, which promises to change the functional food market in Estonia. The new product combines natural ingredients and the latest scientific achievements, including the use of nanotechnology for optimal absorption of nutrients.

What makes EnergyPulse unique?

  • Guava & Superfruit Extract: The drink contains natural guava extract - a source of vitamin C, antioxidants and a mild natural tonic effect.
  • Nanotechnology: The use of innovative technology for encapsulating caffeine and B vitamins ensures their rapid and long-lasting action.
  • Less sugar - more benefits: The drink is sweetened with natural stevia and contains a minimal amount of sugar, which is ideal for those who care about their health.
  • Eco-friendly packaging: The cans are made from recycled aluminium and fully comply with sustainable production standards, highlighting the brand's environmental responsibility.

Local experts' opinion

“EnergyPulse sets a new standard for functional drinks on the Estonian market. It combines natural ingredients and scientific innovations, making it an excellent choice for the people of our country.”

Kristiina Laansalu, a nutritionist from Tartu

When and where to buy?

Starting from December 1, "EnergyPulse" will be available in major chain stores, including Rimi, Selver and Prisma, as well as on popular online platforms. The first buyers will receive special promotions and gifts.

"EnergyPulse" is a new generation of energy for an active life in the rhythm of Estonia!
